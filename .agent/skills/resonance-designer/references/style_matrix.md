# Style Matrix (The 5 Archetypes)

> Do not mix these. Pick one and commit.

## 1. The Brutalist (Power)
*   **Typography**: Huge (8rem+), Uppercase, Condensed.
*   **Border**: 2px-4px Solid Black. No radius (0px).
*   **Palette**: Black, White, Primary (Red/Yellow).
*   **Vibe**: Raw, Truthful, Aggressive.

## 2. The Glassmorphic (Premium)
*   **Typography**: Light weights (300), Wider tracking.
*   **Border**: 1px White (Alpha 0.2). Radius 24px.
*   **Palette**: Dark gradients + Blur (Backdrop Filter).
*   **Vibe**: Futuristic, Expensive, Etheral.

## 3. The Neumorphic (Tactile)
*   **Typography**: Rounded Sans.
*   **Border**: None. Shadows define shape (Light top-left, Dark bottom-right).
*   **Palette**: Low contrast off-white or dark grey.
*   **Vibe**: Soft, Medical, Clean.

## 4. The Swiss (Order)
*   **Typography**: Helvetica/Inter. Strict Hierarchy. Grid-locked.
*   **Border**: 1px Hairline. Radius 0px.
*   **Palette**: White background. Black text. One accent color.
*   **Vibe**: Trustworthy, Academic, Timeless.

## 5. The Cyber (Data)
*   **Typography**: Monospace (JetBrains Mono).
*   **Border**: 1px Terminal Green/Amber.
*   **Palette**: Black background. Neon text. Scanlines.
*   **Vibe**: Hacker, Technical, Dense.
