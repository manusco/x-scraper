# Resonance Style Matrix

> **Objective**: Curated aesthetics for consistent brand identity.

## 1. Interface & Product (UI/UX)
*   **Glassmorphism**: "Frosted glass, blur: 20px, white border 1px, subtle drop shadow, apple aesthetics."
*   **Bento Grid**: "Asymmetric grid layout, modular, card-based design, soft rounded corners, premium spacing."
*   **Claymorphism**: "3D soft clay, inflatable, pastel colors, fluffy, plasticine texture."
*   **Isometric**: "3D isometric view, orthographic camera, low poly or toy-like, studio lighting."

## 2. Photography (Marketing/Lifestyle)
*   **Editorial**: "Vogue magazine style, high fashion, sharp focus, studio backdrop, confident pose."
*   **Candid**: "Shot on Kodak Portra 400, film grain, motion blur, imperfect framing, raw emotion."
*   **Product Shot**: "Floating product, liquid splash, immaculate studio lighting, 8k resolution, macro details."
*   **Cinematic**: "Teal and orange grading, anamorphic lens flares, wide aspect ratio, film look."

## 3. Illustration & Art
*   **Corporate Memphis**: "Flat design, exaggerated proportions, bright solid colors, vector art" (Use sparingly).
*   **Lofi / Anime**: "Studio Ghibli style, watercolor backgrounds, detailed clouds, relaxing vibe."
*   **Pixel Art**: "16-bit, SNES era, dithering, limited color palette."
*   **Line Art**: "Continuous line drawing, minimalist, black ink on white paper."
*   **Ukiyo-e**: "Woodblock print, Hokusai style, mineral pigments, prussian blue and vermilion."

## 4. Texture & Material
*   **Iridescent**: "Holographic, chromatic aberration, oil slick, rainbow reflections."
*   **Matte**: "Rubber, silicone, non-reflective, soft touch."
*   **Metallic**: "Brushed aluminum, gold leaf, oxidized copper, rustic iron."
*   **Organic**: "Wood grain, marble veins, paper fiber, linen texture."
