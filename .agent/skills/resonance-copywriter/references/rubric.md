# The Human Rubric (8 Criteria)

Use this rubric to score content from 1-5. Target > 4.

1.  **Sentence Variance (The Rhythm)**
    *   **1 (Robot)**: All sentences are 15-20 words. Monotone.
    *   **5 (Human)**: Mix of 3-word punches and 30-word flowing thoughts.

2.  **Concrete vs Abstract**
    *   **1 (Robot)**: "We leverage robust solutions to optimize workflow."
    *   **5 (Human)**: "We use a cron job to save you 4 hours a week."

3.  **Opinion/Stance**
    *   **1 (Robot)**: Balanced, neutral, "some say X, others say Y".
    *   **5 (Human)**: Has a distinct point of view. Takes a risk.

4.  **Idiom & Colloquialism**
    *   **1 (Robot)**: Textbook grammar. Zero contractions.
    *   **5 (Human)**: Uses "You know," "Frankly," dashes, and fragments.

5.  **Active Voice**
    *   **1 (Robot)**: "Mistakes were made."
    *   **5 (Human)**: "I messed up."

6.  **Contextual Awareness**
    *   **1 (Robot)**: Explains the obvious.
    *   **5 (Human)**: Respects the reader's intelligence. Skips the basics.

7.  **Sensory Details**
    *   **1 (Robot)**: Conceptual.
    *   **5 (Human)**: Visual, auditory, or tactile words ("Click," "Grind," "Shiny").

8.  **The "So What?" Factor**
    *   **1 (Robot)**: Information dump.
    *   **5 (Human)**: Every paragraph drives towards a specific insight.
