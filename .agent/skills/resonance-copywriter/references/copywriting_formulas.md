# Copywriting Formulas

> **Objective**: Structuring persuasion with proven frameworks.

## 1. The Core Formulas

### PAS (Problem - Agitation - Solution)
*   **Problem**: "Managing multiple projects is a nightmare."
*   **Agitation**: "You miss deadlines, lose clients, and burn out."
*   **Solution**: "Resonance brings order to the chaos in one click."

### AIDA (Attention - Interest - Desire - Action)
*   **Attention**: "Stop losing 20% of your revenue."
*   **Interest**: "Most CRMs leak leads because of poor data entry."
*   **Desire**: "Imagine a CRM that auto-fills itself."
*   **Action**: "Start your free trial today."

### BAB (Before - After - Bridge)
*   **Before**: "You are drowning in spreadsheets."
*   **After**: "You could be finishing work at 5 PM with zero stress."
*   **Bridge**: "Here is how our tool gets you there."

## 2. Headline Formulas (The 4 U's)
1.  **Unique**: "The only CRM built for Underwater Welders."
2.  **Ultra-Specific**: "Save $4,300/year on server costs."
3.  **Urgent**: "Sale ends in 4 hours."
4.  **Useful**: "How to double your leads."
