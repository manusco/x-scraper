# Email Sequence Templates

> **Objective**: Automated relationship building. "Value before Ask."

## 1. Welcome Sequence (The "First Impression")
**Goal**: Activation & Trust.
1.  **Email 1 (Immediate)**: Deliver the Asset + One Action.
2.  **Email 2 (Day 1)**: The Quick Win (Get value in < 5 mins).
3.  **Email 3 (Day 3)**: Origin Story ("Why we built this").
4.  **Email 4 (Day 5)**: Social Proof (Case Study).
5.  **Email 5 (Day 7)**: The Ask (Upgrade/Call).

## 2. Nurture Sequence (The "Long Game")
**Goal**: Top of Mind Awareness.
*   **Framework**: Give, Give, Give, Ask.
*   **Content**: Educational insights, not just product changelogs.

## 3. Abandoned Cart / Recovery
1.  **Email 1 (1 hr)**: "Did you forget something?" (Helpful).
2.  **Email 2 (24 hr)**: Social Proof ("Others love this item").
3.  **Email 3 (48 hr)**: Scarcity/Discount ("Last chance").

## 4. Cold Outreach (B2B)
*   **Subject**: Question about {Company}
*   **Line 1**: "Saw you are leading {Initiative}." (Research).
*   **Line 2**: "We help companies like {Competitor} solve {Problem}." (Value).
*   **Line 3**: "Worth a chat?" (Low friction CTA).
