# Taboo Phrases (The Kill List)

> **Objective**: Eliminate words that scream "LLM-generated".

## Tier 1: Instant Kill (Zero Tolerance)
*   Delve
*   Leverage
*   Harness
*   Tapestry
*   Landscape (in a metaphorical sense)
*   Game-changer
*   Realm
*   Foster (unless referring to a child)
*   Beacon
*   Testament

## Tier 2: The "Fluff" (Delete or Replace)
*   In today's fast-paced world...
*   It is important to note...
*   Seamless / Seamlessly
*   Robust
*   Optimize / Optimization
*   Synergy / Synergistic
*   Empower
*   Elevate
*   Unlock (unless it's a door)
*   Navigate (unless it's a map)

## Tier 3: Structural Tells
*   "Crucial" starting a sentence.
*   "Remember," as a conclusion.
*   "In summary," or "In conclusion,".
